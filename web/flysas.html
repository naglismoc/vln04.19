NEW MEMBERSHIP CONDITIONS
As a result of new EU regulations for handling personal information, we’ve updated our EuroBonus membership conditions. This means that you’ll need to accept the new conditions.

If you want to read the new membership conditions please click here.

When you accept, we’ll be able to send you personalized offers based on information you’ve shared or from our EuroBonus partners. We’ll also analyze how you earn and spend points and preferences, so we can provide you with relevant offers. And we’ll use this data to make EuroBonus even more outstanding.

If you, at this point, choose not to accept the new membership conditions, then the old membership conditions will be valid for some time and you will be reminded.


MANAGING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

Your integrity is vital to us. This is why we always handle your personal information respectfully and do everything we can to protect it.

As a result of new EU regulations for handling personal information, we need to ask for your consent to manage your personal data. How and for what purpose is clearly defined in our new conditions.

If you'd like to read the new conditions, please click here.

When you accept, we'll be able to provide you with relevant and personalized offers based on the information you shared. We'll also analyze what services you prefer and we will use this data to make SAS services even more outstanding

Please note that you can withdraw your consent at any time by logging in and selecting "Manage my subscriptions" in profile settings.
